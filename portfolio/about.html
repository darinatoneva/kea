Hello!

My name is Darina Toneva and I am from Bulgaria. I have been living in Denmark since the summer of 2017. By education I am a Bachelor of Economic Management, but shortly after graduation I decided to devote myself to Graphic Design and I worked as a Graphic Designer for more than ten years. I worked in advertising agencies in Bulgaria, where I had the opportunity to create designs primarily designed for printing on different media and through different technologies. Currently I am a student in Multimedia Design and Communication at KEA - Copenhagen School of Design and Technology.
Why this specialty?
Because it is very closely related to Graphic Design;
Because "the whole world" is already digital and is working increasingly in this direction;
Because it is a great challenge to design something that should be dynamic, to work well and to make people's live easier or just to make them happy.

You can read more about me on LinkedIn or to say "Hi" here: toneva.darina@gmail.com.
