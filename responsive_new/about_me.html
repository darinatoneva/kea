Hello!

My name is Darina Toneva and I am from Bulgaria. I have been living in Denmark since the summer of 2017. By education I am a bachelor of business management but shortly after graduation I decided to devote myself to graphic design and that was my profession for more than ten years.
Currently I am a student in Multimedia Design and Communication at KEA - Copenhagen School of Design and Technology.
Why this specialty? Because it is very closely related to graphic design; because the whole world is already digital and is working increasingly in this direction; because it is a great challenge to design something that should be dynamic, to work well and to make people's lives easier or just to make them happy.
